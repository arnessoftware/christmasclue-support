Privacy Policy – The Christmas Clue

The Christmas Clue does not collect, store, or share any personal data about you.

  • Photos and camera: If you choose to take photos with the camera or save photos in the app, these are processed only locally on your own device. No photos are sent to the developer or third parties.
  • Logs and usage patterns: The app does not generate logs or statistics that can identify you as a user.
  • Network: The app does not communicate with external servers and sends no data outside your device.
  • Cookies and tracking: The app does not use cookies, analytics tools, or any form of tracking.

All information you save or create in The Christmas Clue remains exclusively on your own device.

If you have questions, please contact us at support@arnessoftware.com
